Our THINLINE products are some of the most exciting and innovative natural stone laminate products available today. With an average thickness of just 2mm, these unique sheets of natural stone laminates can transform any mundane surface into an exciting and eye-catching showcase. THINLINE is perfectly suited for interior and exterior application in commercial or residential spaces.

Applications and Installation

  • The timeless beauty of natural stone combined with the ease of installation of laminate sheets.
  • Can install on virtually all existing surfaces, no costly extractions necessary.
  • Thickness maximizes SQFT covered and minimized freight costs.
  • Eco Friendly Production.
  • Malleability allows for simple custom cutting.
  • Installs quickly, cutting all labor costs.
  • Convenient for DIY customers.
